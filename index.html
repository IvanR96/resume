About Me

I’m Ivan Rios, a beginner web developer who’s obsessed with building websites that not only work but actually leave an impression. For me, code isn’t just lines on a screen—it’s a chance to design experiences, tell stories, and make the internet a little more exciting.

I started coding because I was curious, but I stuck with it because I realized it’s the perfect mix of logic and creativity. Whether I’m bringing a design to life, crafting layouts that feel effortless, or experimenting with fresh ideas, I aim to build projects that stand out.

Being new to the industry is actually something I see as an advantage: I’m unafraid to experiment, quick to learn, and hungry to create work that surprises people. My goal? To turn every blank page into something memorable.

If you’re looking for a developer who codes with curiosity, designs with intention, and never stops growing—you just found one.
